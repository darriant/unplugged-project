Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

An adventure is no less grand when met with a map, provided on our about page. There's probably parking somewhere, so good luck.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

If you've got the time and money to experience this retreat, you're in a good place to go for it.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Anywhere between 2 and 20 is a good number for our facilities. Any less and you don't have a team. Any more and some of us won't have a bed.

Is there really no wifi available?

I'm pretty sure there is, but I think the point is to not use it unless you absolutely need to.
